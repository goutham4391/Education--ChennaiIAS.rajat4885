TNPSC Group I Exam Pattern

TNPSC group 1 exam will be conducted in three successive stages namely

  • Preliminary Exam
  • Main Written Exam
  • Interview

he Preliminary Examination is meant to serve as a screening test only. The marks obtained in the Preliminary Examination by the applicants who are declared qualified for admission to the Main Written Examination will not be counted for determining their final order of merit.
