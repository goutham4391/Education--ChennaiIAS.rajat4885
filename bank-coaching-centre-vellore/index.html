IBPS CWE PO & Clerk Examinations

Institute of Banking Personnel Selection (IBPS) conducts the IBPS CWE, PO & Clerk Exams every year. IBPS to select eligible candidates for the position of CWE, PO & Clerk in its various zones all across the country.  Chennai IAS Academy is one the Best IBPS PO & Clerk Exam Coaching Center in Vellore.

IBPS PO & Clerk will be conducted in 4 different Phases:

  • Preliminary Exam
  • Mains Exam
  • Document Verification
  • Medical Examination

he Preliminary Examination is meant to serve as a screening test only. The marks obtained in the Preliminary Examination by the applicants who are declared qualified for admission to the Main Written Examination will not be counted for determining their final order of merit.

IBPS PO & Clerk Exam Selection Process

Candidates opting for IBPS PO & Clerk Exam should know the Selection Procedure of the recruitment.  Students will be selected on the basis of the marks scored in prelims & mains stage whereas other stages will be qualifying in nature.

Age Limits:

The minimum age of the candidates must be 20 years and the maximum age limit is 28 years, to be eligible for the upcoming IBPS PO & Clerk Exams.

For SC/ST candidates: 05 Years | For OBC candidates: 03 Years.

SSC CGL Exam Pattern Tier IV

Tier IV is going to be of qualifying nature, here no leniency will be given to any category candidate, if you qualify it according to the standards set then you will be selected. In this part your Data Entry Skill Test (DEST) will be taken, where your typing speed will be tested and the required criteria is 8000 key depressions per hour. Next your Computer Proficiency Test will be conducted, which is divided into three modules, that is, Word Processing, Spread Sheet and Generation of Slides, for the posts of Assistant Section Officer of CSS, Assistant Section Officer (MEA), Assistant in Serious Fraud Investigation Office (SFIO) under the Ministry of Corporate Affairs and Assistant (GSI) in the Ministry of Mines. You should have good knowledge of all these.
